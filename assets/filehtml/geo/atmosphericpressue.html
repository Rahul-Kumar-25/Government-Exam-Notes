Atmospheric Pressure

Air Pressure: Total weight of a mass of a column of air per unit area at sea level.

Instrument used to measure: Barometer
Unit: millibar (mb)

Atmospheric pressure is max at sea level and it ↓ with ↑ in height or altitude.

Isobar

Line connecting point having equal value of pressure.

Factor that affect Pressure

  • Temp. → Pressure ∝ 1 / Temp.
  • Rotation → Earth rotates, centrifugal force, air deflects, causing ↓ in pressure.
  • Water vapour → Air with higher quantity of water vapour = low pressure
    Molecular weight of water < molecular weight of gas or air
    Humid air less pressure than dry air
