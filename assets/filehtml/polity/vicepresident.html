Polity - Vice-President

Vice President

I. Constitutional Status

  • Second highest constitutional office
  • Office is modeled on American Vice President

II. Role & Functions

  1. Act as President during vacancy or absence
  2. He/She is ex-officio Chairman of Raj Sabha
  3. Maintain order and decorum in Raj Sabha
  4. Vote in case of tie in Raj Sabha (casting vote)
  5. Appoint chairman and members of Raj Sabha committees

III. Elections

  • Indirect election
  • Elected by elected members plus nominated of LS and RS
  • Single transferable vote
  • Secret ballot
  • Dispute → Supreme Court

IV. Qualifications

  • Citizen of India
  • Age – 35 years
  • Qualified as member of Raj Sabha
  • Must not hold any office of profit

V. Oath

By President or authorised person

VI. Term & Vacancy

  • 5 years
  • Resign anytime
  • Eligible for re-election for any number of term

Vacancy

  • Remove
  • Resign
  • Death
  • Tenure
  • Disqualification

Removal

  • Effective majority in Raj Sabha
  • Simple majority in Lok Sabha
  • 14 day prior notice in Raj Sabha
  • No impeachment is required
