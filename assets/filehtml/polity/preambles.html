Basic - Preambles

Preamble

  • it’s kind of index of the constitution
  • It is a kind introduction or preface of constitution
  • first borrowed from American constitution (US)
  • N.A. Palkhival – said preamble / constitution as identity card
  • formally adopted – 22 Jan 1947
  • officially or actually – 26 Nov, 1949
  • who designed preamble – Beohar Ram Manohar Singha
  • Amendment – 1976 (42nd Amendment Act)
    • Socialist
    • Secular
    • Integrity

Ingredients of Preamble

  1. Source of Authority – people of India
  2. Nature of Indian state – Sovereign, Socialist, Secular, Democratic and Republic.

Objective of Constitution

Justice → Liberty → Equality → Fraternity

Key words of Preamble

  1. Sovereign → not interfere in external and internal affairs.
  2. Socialist → Democratic Socialist
  3. Secular → positive secular
  4. Democratic
    • Direct (supreme power in hands of people)
    • Indirect (✓) In India, indirect democratic
  5. Republic → Head of state elect करके चुनते हैं
  6. Justice
    • Social
    • Economic
    • Political
  7. Liberty → thoughts, expressions, faith, belief and worship.
  8. Equality → (absence of special privileges)
    • Civic
    • Politics
    • Economic
  9. Fraternity → DPSP (51A) through unity and integrity of nation.

Is Preamble part of Constitution or Not :

  • Berubari Union case 1969 :
    • not part of constitution
  • Kesavananda Bharti case 1973 :
    • It’s integral part of constitution
  • LIC 1995 – preamble is a part of constitution

Important Points

  • Preamble is a part of constitution
  • It is non justiciable (not go to the supreme court regarding any)
  • Preamble is neither source of power to legislature nor prohibition
  • Upon the power of legislature
  • Basic structure – is not mentioned in the constitution
