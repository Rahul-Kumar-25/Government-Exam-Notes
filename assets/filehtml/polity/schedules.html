Basic - Schedules

Schedules (12)

Schedule I (States & UTs)

contain the name of states and union territories along with their territorial jurisdiction.

Schedule II (Salaries, allowances of presidents)

outlines the provisions for the salaries, allowances and privileges of president, governor, judge and other officials.

Schedule III (Oath & affirmation)

Details the oaths and affirmation for constitutional post like president & members of parliament.

Schedule IV (Seats for states & UTs)

Specify the allocation of seats for states & union territories in the council of Rajya Sabha (State).

Note: Council of state is called the Rajya Sabha.

Schedule V (ST) Schedule Areas of administration

Provide the administration and control of Schedule areas and schedule tribes.

Schedule VI (S.T / S.A But in specific states)

Related to the administration of tribal areas in the states of Assam, Meghalaya, Tripura, Mizoram.

Schedule VII (it talks about subject of union, state & concurrent)

  • Union list
  • State
  • Concurrent (Both union & state)

Divides subjects into three list → union list, state list, concurrent list, detailing the distribution of power b/w central & state govt.

Schedule VIII (Official languages)

Enumerates the official languages recognised by constitution.

Note: 22 official languages

Schedule IX (Judicial Review)

Contains laws that are exempt from judicial review, primarily related to land reforms, added by First Amendment in 1951 (CAA).

Schedule X – Defection (Anti-defection)

(when party MLA chhodkar dusri party me jana, they are disqualified)

Provisions regarding disqualifications on the grounds of defection.

Added by 52nd CAA, 1985

Schedule XI (Panchayati Raj)

Defines the powers authorities and responsibilities of Panchayati Raj institution (Local self govt.)

Added by 73rd Amendment Act 1992

Schedule XII (Municipalities)

Specifies the powers, authorities and responsibilities of municipalities.

Added by 74th Amendment Act 1992
