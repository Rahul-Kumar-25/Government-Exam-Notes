Polity

Union - President

President (Union)

Union

  • President
  • Tenure → 5 years
  • First citizen of India
  • Acts as unity, integrity and solidarity of India
  • Elections → Indirect

Electoral College

  • LS + RS + LA
  • 2 members nominated
  • Legislative Assembly
  • Single Transferable Vote
  • Secret Ballot

Representation

  • Centre → LS → RS
  • State → LA → LC
  • LA → UT case: Delhi & Puducherry

Disputes

  • Supreme Court

LA Voting Calculation

  • MLA = Total Population × 1 / 1000
  • Elected members of LA

Election Formula

  • MLA = P / E × 1000
  • LS MP =
    • Total value of votes of all MLA of all states
    • Total elected MPs
  • Total votes of valid votes ÷ (1 + 1)

Qualifications

  • Citizen of India
  • Age ≥ 35 years
  • Qualified as member of Lok Sabha
  • Must not hold office of profit

Conditions

  • If holding any seat, must not be member of Parliament or State Legislature
  • If elected as President, seat is deemed vacated
  • Should not hold any other office of profit
  • Official residence: Rashtrapati Bhawan
  • Entitled to allowances and privileges determined by Parliament

Salary & Allowances

  • Cannot be diminished during term of office

Vacancy

  • Death
  • Resignation
  • Tenure completion
  • Impeachment

Succession

  • Vice-President
  • President (until new election)
  • Chief Justice of India
  • Senior Judge of Supreme Court

Impeachment

  • Ground: Constitution violation
  • Initiated by either LS or RS
  • Charge signed by 1/4th members
  • 14 days prior notice to President

Impeachment Process

  • LS initiates
  • Voting
  • 2nd House (RS)
  • 2/3rd majority of total members required
  • Nominated members can participate

Oath

  • Administered by Chief Justice of India

Powers of President

Administrative Powers

  • Appoints UPSC Chairman & Members
  • Attorney General of India
  • CAG of India
  • Chief Justice & Judges of Supreme Court
  • Chief Justice & Judges of High Courts
  • Chief Election Commissioner & other Election Commissioners
  • Members of Inter-State Council
  • Chief Commissioner of Union Territories
  • Finance Commission Members
  • Language Commission Members
  • Backward Classes Commission Members
  • Minority Commission Members
  • Indian Ambassadors & Diplomats
  • All executive actions taken in President’s name (Article 77)
  • All Union officers are subordinate to President
  • Right to be informed of affairs of Union

Judicial Powers

  • Pardon, reprieve, respite
  • Remission, suspension or commutation of sentence
  • Court martial cases
  • Offence against Union law
  • Death sentence cases

Military Powers

  • Supreme commander of defence forces
  • Parliament regulates power (Article 53(2))
  • Parliament approval needed for expenditure

Emergency Powers

  • National Emergency
  • State Emergency
  • Financial Emergency

Diplomatic Powers

  • Negotiates treaties on advice of ministers
  • Represents India internationally
  • Appoints Indian representatives abroad

Legislative Powers

  • Summon or prorogue Parliament
  • Dissolve Lok Sabha
  • Summon joint sitting in deadlock
  • Addresses Parliament after elections and yearly session
  • Can address either house or joint sitting
  • in the RS 12 members nominated
  • President’s prior recommendation required for:
    • Formation of new states (Article 3)
    • Money Bills
    • Bills affecting taxation
    • Bills restricting trade (Article 304a)

Bill Assent Powers

  • Give assent
  • Withhold assent
  • Return bill (except Money Bill)
  • Promulgate ordinance when Parliament not in session
