History

Delhi Sultanate

Delhi Sultanate (1206 - 1526 CE)

Introduction: It was a Muslim rule in India establish after the decline of Gori Empire.

  • capital - Delhi
  • period - 1206 - 1526 CE
  • founded by Qutbuddin Aibak
  • ended with Babar's victory over Ibrahim Lodi in the first battle of Panipat (1526)
  • five dynasty of Delhi Sultanate :
  1. Slave Dynasty : (1206 - 1290)
  2. Khilji dynasty : (1290 - 1320)
  3. Tughlaq dynasty : (1320 - 1414)
  4. Saiyyad dynasty : (1414 - 1451)
  5. Lodhi dynasty : (1451 - 1526)

1. Slave Dynasty (1206 - 1290)

Qutbuddin Aibak (1206 - 1210)

  1. founder of Delhi Sultanate
  2. former slave of Mohammad Ghori
  3. known as "Lakh Baksh" (Giver of Lakhs)
  4. started construction of Kutub minar and Quwwat-ul-Islam mosque
  5. died while playing chaugam
  6. capital - Lahore

Iltutmish (1211 - 1236)

  1. Real consolidator of Sultanate
  2. shifted capital from Lahore to Delhi
  3. introduced Iqta system and silver Tanka and copper Jital
  4. completed Kutub minar
  5. got Caliph's recognition
  6. organized Chahalgani (40 Noble Groups)
  7. father of Tomb building (Sultangarhi)

Raziy a Sultan (1236 - 1239)

  1. first and only women ruler
  2. faced opposition due to gender
  3. appointed Yakut to important office of Amir-e-Akhur
  4. died in rebellion

Balban (1266 - 1287)

  1. introduced theory of kingship
  2. Practices :
    • Zil-i-Ilahi
    • Sajda
    • Pibos (Kissing Sultan's feet)
  3. Strengthen monarchy
  4. Suppressed Nobles and Mongols

Kaikubad (1287 - 1290)

  1. Grandson of Balban
  2. Later Jalaluddin Khilji took a bold step by murdering them and seized the throne.

2. Khilji Dynasty (1290 - 1320)

Jalaluddin Khilji (1290 - 1296)

  1. founder
  2. Known for lenient policies
  3. He was first Sultan of Delhi Sultanate to have benevolent attitude towards Hindus
  4. Provided harsh punishments

Alauddin Khilji (1296 - 1316)

  1. first Sultan to :
    • conquer South India
    • maintain permanent standing Army
  2. Reforms :
    • market control system
    • price control on food, cloths, Horses etc.
    • land Revenue fixed at 50%
  3. Abolished - Iqta Hereditary rights
  4. He reversed policy of tolerance
  5. Introduced Branding of Horse (Dagh) and Chehra system
  6. Successfully resisted mongol Invasion
  7. Temple of Somnath was captured and destroyed
  8. General malik kafur led Deccan expedition
  9. He patronised poet like Amir Khusrau and Mir Hasan Dehlvi
  10. first ruler to fix land revenue in cash

Qutbuddin Mubarak Shah

  • Revoked all harsh regulations

Nasiruddin Khusrau Shah

3. Tughlaq Dynasty (1320 - 1414)

Ghiyasuddin Tughlaq (1320 - 1325)

  1. founder
  2. Took the title "Ghazi" (Slayer of infidels)
  3. Build Tughlaqabad fort
  4. first Sultan to start irrigation work
  5. Died mysteriously

Mohammad bin Tughlaq (1325 - 1351)

  1. most controversial ruler
  2. famous for five experiment :
    • transfer of capital Delhi → Devagiri
    • Token currency (copper coins)
    • Taxation in Doab (rise the land Revenue)
    • Khurasan Expedition
    • Qarachil Expedition
  3. Highly educated but impracticable
  4. Revenue increase
  5. set a new department of Agriculture that is "Diwan-i-AmirKohi"
  6. Built the fort of Adilabad & city of Jahanpana

Firozshah Tughlaq (1351 - 1388)

  1. welfare Ruler
  2. Build canals, Hospitals & madarsa
  3. Reduce taxes, Abolished torture, promoted Slavery system
  4. Revive Iqtadari system and also made it Hereditary
  5. first Sultan to impose Sharb (Irrigation Tax)
  6. Introduced two new coins (Adha & Bital)
  7. New Department (Diwan-e-Khairat) (Department of Charity)
  8. Patronised scholar like Barani who wrote "Tarikh-i-Firozshah" & "Futua-i-Jahandari"
  9. Khwaja Abdul Malik Islami wrote book Futuh-us-Salatin
  10. He wrote himself Futuhat-e-Firozshahi

Daulat Khan

  • Defeated by Khizr Khan

4. Saiyyad Dynasty (1414 - 1451)

  • founded by Khizr Khan
  • claimed descent from prophet Mohd.
  • weak rulers
  • controlled only Delhi Region
  • Taimur invasion weaken the Sultanate
  1. Khizr Khan (1414 - 1421)
  2. Mubarak Shah (1421 - 1434)
    • first Sultan to appoint the Hindu Noble
  3. Mohamad Shah (1434 - 1443)
  4. Alauddin Alam Shah (1443 - 1451)
    • invited Bahlol Lodhi to take charge of the Army

5. Lodhi Dynasty (1451 - 1526)

Last ruling family of Sultanate period & first to be headed by Afghans who were ruling over Sirhind

Bahlol Lodhi (1451 - 1489)

  • founder
  • Afgan Ruler, introduced Bahlol copper coin

Sikander Lodhi (1489 - 1517)

  • founder Agra
  • promoted Agriculture
  • showed little tolerance toward non-muslims
  • Re-imposed Jaziya on non-muslims
  • Introduce Gaz-i-Sikandari (system of auditing of Accounts)
  • He destroyed many Hindu temples & imposed many restrictions.

Ibrahim Lodhi (1517 - 1526)

  • last Sultan
  • Arrogant & Repressive
  • openly insulted & humilated his Nobles
  • Daulat Khan Lodi, the Governor of Punjab invited Babar to invade India.
  • Babar defeated and kill Ibrahim Lodhi in the first Battle of Panipat (1526)
